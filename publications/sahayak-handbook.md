## Visual & Design Notes

The Sahayak is a heavily illustrated, print-first publication. Its visual language carries as much meaning as the text:

- **Illustration style:** Grainy, textured botanical illustrations in Devalok's signature Padmavarna (#D33163) pink tones — lotus flowers in various stages (bud, bloom, seed pod), hands in mudras, fruits on branches (mangoes, oranges), magnolia blossoms
- **Recurring motifs:** Hands reaching, picking, holding — representing the act of crafting. Flowers in stages of bloom — representing the creation cycle. The Svadhisthana six-petaled flower pattern appears on endpapers and as a section marker throughout.
- **Color treatment:** Padmavarna pink is the dominant accent. Section backgrounds rotate between pink, Padmavarna (solid), red/terracotta (for Print & Event), blue (for UIUX), dark green (for Packaging), and neutral/warm tones for case studies.
- **Typography:** Display headings in large Bricolage Grotesque Bold, body text in Inter. Sanskrit text rendered in large Devanagari display type.
- **Physical qualities:** Hardbound, velvet-touch finish, intentionally heavy in the hand. The tactility is part of the brand experience — "the impact is completely different altogether" when held physically.

### Packaging Design — I

*[Dark green background, white text. Accompanied by Eat Purposefully "Arizona Sunshine" granola packaging showing functional icons, brand placement, product photography, information panels, and nutritional details]*

1. **Functional Icons** — Simple visual cues that highlight key product benefits like vegan, gluten-free, or organic, building instant trust and appeal.

2. **Variant Design** — Clear callouts, colors, and illustrations that help shoppers quickly identify and remember their preferred option.

3. **Information Hierarchy** — The arrangement of name, product, and flavor that directs attention and makes the pack easy to read at a glance.

4. **Materials** — Choices in pouch, box, or label finishes that affect usability, shelf presence, and how premium the product feels.

5. **Brand Device** — A blend of words and symbols placed on the pack to add warmth and create small moments of delight for the customer.

---

### Packaging Design — II (Customized/Structural)

*[Pink/mauve background. Accompanied by Bondle baby sleep sack packaging dieline — a full die-cut template on kraft paper showing structural folds, closure systems, QR codes, safety guidelines, and size guides]*

1. **Dieline** — A flat template that guides cuts, folds, and artwork, ensuring your packaging is accurate, functional, and production-ready.

2. **Closure System** — The custom flap/lock style at the top and bottom that secures the product and adds to a delightful unboxing experience.

3. **Information Panels** — Dedicated panels for size guides, safety info, care instructions, or certifications that build trust and comply with regulations.

4. **Additional Add Ons** — Clear placement for key details like dates, size, and ingredients, with optional stickers or labels that make your packaging stand out on shelves.

5. **Mascot/character design** — A custom mascot designed for your brand finds its place on the packaging, adding personality and creating a familiar face your customers connect and interact with.

### UIUX Design

*[Blue background, white text. Accompanied by MoveCars.com website redesign — showing search UI component evolution, logo progression (red arrow mark), and full laptop mockup of the live website]*

1. **Information Architecture** — Layouts, typography, and visual grouping that make complex content easy to scan, understand, and trust.

2. **User Flow & Journey** — Step-by-step structures, expandable sections, and CTAs that guide users from discovery to action without friction.

3. **Visual System** — Unified color palettes, typography, icons, cards and grids that ensure brand consistency and recognition across the product.

4. **Core UI Elements** — Buttons, menus, forms, cards, icons, and banners designed to be functional, consistent, and intuitive across every screen.

5. **Responsive Design** — Interfaces crafted to adapt seamlessly to desktop, tablet, and mobile, ensuring clarity at every scale.

6. **Prototyping & Handoff** — Interactive mockups and developer-ready assets that bridge the gap from design to build, keeping execution precise.

*Selected Work: MoveCars.com — website redesign for a 28-year-old domain. [dvlk.in/movecars](https://dvlk.in/movecars)*

## Our Process

*[Section opens with botanical illustration: Magnolia/lotus flowers in three stages of bloom — bud, opening, and full flower — representing the creative process unfolding]*

> Everything starts with a **CONVERSATION**.
> A conversation, leads to **UNDERSTANDING**.
> And understanding, leads to **CREATION**.

*[Each step number is decorated with botanical flower illustrations integrated into the numeral]*

### 1. Discovery Call

Every partnership begins with a conversation. We meet to exchange questions, listen openly, and share expectations. You leave with a clear sense of whether we're the right fit to move forward together.

### 2. Onboarding and Brand Workshop

We welcome you into our process with a workshop designed for your brand. Together, we dive deep into its story, values, and creative direction. We gain sharper insight into what the brand stands for & where it should go.

### 3. Creative Development

With a strong foundation in place, we begin exploring ideas. Concepts are developed, tested, and refined with your feedback at every step. The process ensures the final direction feels true and crafted for impact.

### 4. Brand Applications

A brand comes alive only when it reaches people across touchpoints. We extend the identity into websites, packaging, campaigns, and experiences. This ensures that you have a brand ecosystem that works seamlessly wherever it shows up.

### 5. Official Brand Launch

The launch is the moment your brand meets its audience for the first time. We prepare every asset, platform, and channel so that the rollout feels complete and consistent. You step forward with a presence that is distinct, memorable, and ready to grow.
